PAYDAY2

Released: 13 August 2013
Overkill Software

Payday 2 is a cooperative first-person shooter video game developed by Overkill Software and published by 505 Games. The game is a sequel to 2011's Payday: The Heist. Two years after the events of the previous game, a new gang comes to the Washington, D.C. area to perform another heisting spree. The player takes control of one of the gang's 21 members and can perform hists alone, or with up to 3 other teammates. The player can participate in a variety of heists, including but not limited to robbing banks.
